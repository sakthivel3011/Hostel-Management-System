About Our Company

Our Story

SRI ANNAKAMATCHI TRADERS was established in January 2015 with a commitment to delivering high quality rice at the best value. We source paddy directly from farmers in Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh, ensuring freshness and supporting local agriculture.

Founded by Mr. P.T. Singaravel, B.Com, we prioritize quality, fair pricing, and customer satisfaction above all else. Our modern milling facility ensures that every grain meets our strict quality standards.
