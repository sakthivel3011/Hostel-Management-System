Facilities

Accommodation

Spacious and well-ventilated rooms accommodating 2 to 4 students, equipped with basic amenities.

Dining

Nutritious and hygienic vegetarian meals served in separate dining halls for boys and girls.

Recreation

Common rooms with indoor games, television, and reading materials for relaxation and entertainment.

Sports

Facilities for various sports including basketball, volleyball, badminton, and table tennis.

Medical

On-campus dispensary with qualified doctors available for medical assistance.

Internet

24/7 Wi-Fi connectivity across the hostel premises for academic and research purposes.
